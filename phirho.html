Phi Sigma Rho Website Redesign /

Design

Spring 2017. In this project, we were responsible for redesigning and updating UW’s chapter of Phi Sigma Rho’s website. To keep the website friendly to those who aren’t familiar with coding in the future, we used Wix to manage the design template we created. We redesigned all of the website’s pages and currently maintain all content so it’s relevant to current happenings in the sorority.
