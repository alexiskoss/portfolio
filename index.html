Alexis Koss

Hello, I'm currently a junior in Informatics at the University of Washington. I grew up on the eastern side of Washington in a town called Ritzville that only has a population around 1,000. To expand my horizon, I decided that I wanted to move to the big, rainy city of Seattle to start my life and future career here.

Dabbling in code when I was younger is what got me hooked to pursue a career in the technology field today.

Some fun facts about me include:

  • I love cats. I have four at home.
  • Iced caramel macchiatos from Starbucks are my favorite drink.
  • Fall is my favorite season. Who doesn't love pumpkin spice flavored everything?
  • Rain is my favorite weather, it's part of the reason why I moved to Seattle!
  • Photography is one of my favorite hobbies.
