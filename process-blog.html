Writing

Autumn 2015. Throughout HCDE 210, all of us kept a process blog to keep track of our progress and improvements throughout the course.

Each week I wrote a summary of what I did plus I also included pictures of the projects I competed. By doing this, I was able to reflect each week on the new principles or skills I learned, such as data visualization, device prototyping, or even usability research. I even thought back on the things I found difficult so in the future when the problem arises again, I can manage it better or even avoid it completely. By thinking about how the principles and skills I learned apply to the rest of the world, I now am able to think about design problems from multiple different perspectives.
